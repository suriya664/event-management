Our Story

A decade of innovation and collaboration

How It Started

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate technologists in Bangalore, EventPro began as a small meetup of 200 developers. Our vision was simple: create a space where Indian tech professionals could learn, share, and grow together.

Growth & Evolution

Over the years, EventPro has evolved into India's most anticipated technology conference. We've hosted over 50,000 attendees, featured 500+ speakers, and facilitated countless partnerships and innovations that have shaped the Indian tech ecosystem.

Our Impact

EventPro has become a launchpad for startups, a networking hub for professionals, and a learning platform for students. Many of India's successful tech ventures and collaborations have their roots in connections made at our conferences.

Looking Ahead

As we step into 2025, our commitment remains unchanged: to provide the best platform for technology professionals to connect, learn, and innovate. Join us in shaping the future of technology in India and beyond.
